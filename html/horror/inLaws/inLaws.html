Plot

The story revolves around a small coastal town called Harmony Harbor, known for its peaceful and idyllic atmosphere. However, the town's tranquility is shattered when a giant, man-eating in-law descends upon the unsuspecting residents.
Meet Jerry Fintooth, an overbearing and eccentric father-in-law who comes to visit his son-in-law, Dave Waters, and his family. Jerry is not your typical in-law; he's larger than life, opinionated, and seems to create chaos wherever he goes. As he overstays his welcome, the town begins to experience strange occurrences, from comically exaggerated family drama to absurd situations.
When local authorities and the Waters family realize that Jerry is the cause of the chaos, they embark on a mission to deal with the in-law menace. The town's quirky characters, including the bumbling mayor, a witty marine biologist, and a reluctant sheriff, must come together to outwit and confront the monstrous in-law before he devours the peace and harmony of Harmony Harbor.
