Plot

Set in a quirky and eccentric hotel known for its bizarre culinary experiences, the film follows the misadventures of the Hapgood family as they take over the hotel for the off-season. Jack Hapgood, a struggling food critic, accepts a job as the winter caretaker, hoping to find inspiration for his next big review. However, as the family settles in, they quickly discover that the hotel has a dark and hilarious side.
As Jack becomes obsessed with creating the perfect dish, his mental state begins to deteriorate, leading to a series of absurd and surreal events. The hotel's culinary staff, including the mysterious head chef Grizzlegrub, adds to the chaos with their peculiar cooking techniques and odd ingredients. Meanwhile, Jack's son, Danny, starts having bizarre visions of food-related horrors that hint at the hotel's twisted past.
The film combines elements of horror and satire, with culinary mishaps and surreal comedic moments driving the narrative. As Jack descends into madness, the hotel's culinary creations come to life in bizarre and humorous ways, turning the once-hospitable establishment into a culinary nightmare.
