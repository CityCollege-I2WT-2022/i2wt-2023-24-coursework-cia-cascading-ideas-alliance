Plot

In the mystical land of Alecadabra, a once-respected wizard named Merlot the Magnificent has fallen from grace and turned to the dark side of the spirits. He discovers a forbidden potion that allows him to control the magical properties of alcohol, turning him into the infamous "Boozard." With his newfound powers, Boozard plans to intoxicate the entire kingdom and rule over a land filled with drunk citizens.
Desperate to save Alecadabra from this tipsy tyrant, a young and ambitious wizard apprentice named Chardonnay sets out on a quest to find the legendary "Sobriety Stone." Along the way, Chardonnay encounters a quirky group of companions, including a sassy talking beer mug, a clumsy wine cork, and a grumpy whiskey barrel.
As they journey through vineyards, breweries, and distilleries, facing challenges and comedic mishaps, Chardonnay must outsmart Boozard and restore balance to Alecadabra before the kingdom is forever under the influence of the Wizard of Booze.
