Plot

In a world where office supplies rule and the workplace is a battleground, the once mighty hero John Weak finds himself on the brink of retirement. However, when the nefarious Eraserface emerges with a diabolical plan to erase the entire office building and its employees from existence, John Weak is reluctantly pulled back into action.
Eraserface, armed with a giant eraser capable of wiping out anything in its path, is working under the mysterious Boss, who seeks to eliminate all competition in the stationery industry. The Boss has enlisted the help of the seductive and deadly Miss Filing Cabinet to carry out the finer details of their sinister plot.
Realizing he can't face this threat alone, John Weak assembles a team of unlikely allies to save the day. He recruits the tech-savvy IT Guy, Ethan Unplugged, the energetic Coffee Guy, Brews Willis, the efficient but deadly Secretary, Scarlett Paperclip, and the wise-cracking Janitor.
As the team embarks on a hilarious and action-packed mission to stop Eraserface and The Boss, they encounter office supply-themed obstacles, from paper cuts to stapler shootouts. Each member of the team brings their unique skills to the table, with John Weak discovering that true strength comes from the unlikeliest of places.
In a climactic showdown, the team faces off against Eraserface and The Boss in an epic battle for the fate of the office world. With a mix of over-the-top action sequences and comedic twists, "John Weak" delivers a parody adventure that leaves the audience laughing and cheering for the most unlikely hero of them all. Will John Weak and his team save the day, or will the eraser of doom wipe them out for good?
