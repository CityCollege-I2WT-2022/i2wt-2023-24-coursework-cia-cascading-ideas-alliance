Average-rs

Year Language Running time
2012 English 2:29:50

Rating: ★★★

This film's relentless satire keeps the laughs coming, with a clever script and spot-on performances. Average-rs is a pitch-perfect parody that hits all the right notes.

Plot

In the bustling city of Subparville, an unlikely group of individuals with mundane superpowers discover their destinies as they are brought together to form the "Average-rs." Chad Thunderweak, a mild-mannered office worker, becomes the unintentional leader known as "Captain Mild Manner." They are summoned by a mysterious figure named Mediocre Man, who believes that the city needs a team of average heroes to save it from an impending threat.
The villainous Dr. Perfectionist is plotting to impose perfection upon Subparville, eliminating all imperfections and quirks. To combat this threat, the Average-rs must overcome their own flaws and learn to work together.
As the Average-rs attempt to train and harness their mediocre powers, they face hilarious mishaps and misunderstandings. Their first attempt at teamwork results in a disaster, causing chaos in the city. Despite their flaws, they slowly learn to appreciate each other's uniqueness and start embracing their mediocrity.
The climax sees the Average-rs facing off against Dr. Perfectionist and his army of perfectly synchronized henchmen. In a surprising turn of events, their individual quirks and shortcomings prove to be the key to defeating the villain. The battle is filled with comedic moments as the team fumbles through their powers.
In the end, Subparville is saved not by perfection, but by the charm of the Average-rs and their acceptance of their own flaws. The city learns to celebrate mediocrity, realizing that imperfections make life interesting and enjoyable. The Average-rs, though still average in their abilities, become local heroes and continue to protect Subparville from everyday threats.
